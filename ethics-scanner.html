The Anatomy of an ICAI Violation

Most CA firms don't intentionally violate the code. The violations occur when standard marketing agencies try to force SEO rankings using generic retail tactics. Here is what our engine scans for:

Comparative Superlatives

Words like "Best", "Top", "Leading", or "No. 1" are strictly prohibited. They imply superiority over other ICAI members, which constitutes professional misconduct under the First Schedule.

Audit Solicitation

While the 2026 rules allow "Push Technology" for advisory services, directly soliciting Statutory Audit or Assurance work via digital platforms remains an immediate, flaggable offense.

YMYL Failures

While not an ICAI violation, failing Google's Your Money or Your Life (YMYL) standards destroys your traffic. We scan for missing E-E-A-T Schema and authoritative entity linking.
