The Local Engine

How We Capture the Map Pack

Local SEO is an exact science. We meticulously optimize your firm's digital entity to signal absolute local relevance to Google's crawlers.

NAPP Consistency

We audit and synchronize your firm's Name, Address, Phone, and Professional Credentials across 50+ tier-1 business directories. Any discrepancy in address formats across the web confuses Google and drops your local ranking.

Service Taxonomy

We completely rebuild your GMB services menu. Instead of just listing "Tax Services," we inject localized, compliant sub-categories like "GST Registration in [City]" and "ROC Filing," creating direct matches for specific client searches.

Ethical Review Velocity

Google heavily weights review frequency. We implement compliant, automated systems to request reviews from satisfied consultancy clients (adhering to ICAI non-solicitation rules), steadily building your local "Trust Score".

Managing Multiple Branch Offices?

Firms with pan-India presence require complex local architectures to prevent branch profiles from cannibalizing each other in search.
