The Paradigm Shift

"Push" vs. "Pull" Technology

For decades, CAs were strictly limited to "Pull" technology—passive websites that a user had to actively search for.

As of April 1, 2026, the 13th Edition Code of Ethics officially distinguishes between exclusive assurance services and non-exclusive advisory services, explicitly permitting "Push" technology (direct advertising, PPC, proactive digital outreach) for the latter.

Pull Marketing

Passive

Strategies where the client actively seeks the professional. Permitted for all services, including Statutory Audit.

  • Search Engine Optimization (SEO)
  • Google My Business (Local SEO)
  • Technical Tax Flashes / Blogs

Push Marketing

Active

Strategies proactively delivered to the client. Strictly prohibited for Audit/Assurance. Permitted for Advisory.

  • Google Search PPC Ads
  • LinkedIn Targeted Ads
  • Email Newsletter Outreach

Copywriting Governance

Our 50-Point Ethics Filter

Every meta-tag, headline, ad copy, and blog post we write is passed through an internal governance checklist to ensure zero solicitation violations.

The Red Lines (Prohibited)

  • Superlatives & Comparisons We never use words like "Best," "Top," "Cheapest," "Leading," or "Premier" anywhere on your website or ad copy.
  • Guaranteed Outcomes We never promise specific financial results (e.g., "100% Tax Savings Guaranteed" or "Guaranteed GST Refund in 7 Days").
  • Unverifiable Client Testimonials We do not publish client reviews or logos without explicit, documented consent and compliance verification.
  • Soliciting Audit Work We never use "Push" marketing (emails, Google Ads, LinkedIn Ads) to directly ask for statutory audit or attest engagements.

The Green Lines (Permitted)

  • Factual & Descriptive Keywords We use precise, locational keywords: "Chartered Accountants in [City]," "GST Consultants," "Transfer Pricing Advisors."
  • Technical Thought Leadership We heavily promote your firm through educational content, Technical Tax Flashes, and analyses of new government circulars.
  • Factual Achievements We state verifiable facts: "Established in 1995," "Team of 15 Partners," or "Experience in Manufacturing Sector Compliance."
  • Push Marketing for Advisory We aggressively use Google/LinkedIn Ads to generate leads for explicitly permitted non-exclusive services like virtual CFO and incorporation.
