The B2C Trap

Why your firm's blog generates zero ROI.

When CA firms hire standard marketing agencies, the agencies outsource the content creation to junior, generalist copywriters. The result? Elementary articles titled "What is a PAN Card?" or "Top 5 Benefits of GST."

This is a catastrophic waste of capital. A corporate director looking for a Virtual CFO or Transfer Pricing advice does not need GST defined for them. By publishing B2C retail content, you actively signal to high-net-worth clients that your firm does not operate at their level.

Our Methodology

The Google Discover Engine

Standard SEO waits for someone to search. We trigger Google's proactive recommendation algorithm (Google Discover) to push your firm's analyses directly to the mobile phones of corporate executives.

Daily Portal Scraping

Our systems monitor government portals (Income Tax, GSTN, MCA). The moment a high-impact notification or tribunal ruling is published, it enters our editorial queue.

Finance-Led Drafting
